🔍 Recognising Drug Use in Others

Recognising signs of drug abuse in someone you care about is the first step to helping them. These signs span physical, behavioural, and social changes.

CategorySigns to Look For
PhysicalSudden weight loss/gain, neglected hygiene, unusual body odour, red eyes, track marks on arms
BehaviouralMood swings, increased secrecy, irritability, erratic behaviour, missing school/work
SocialWithdrawing from family/friends, new suspicious friends, abandoning previous interests
PsychologicalParanoia, extreme anxiety, sudden euphoria followed by depression
FinancialMissing money at home, selling personal items, frequently borrowing money
🇰🇪 Grace, Nakuru: Noticed her younger brother Benson missing money from the kitchen, coming home with red eyes, and abandoning his football team — things he loved. She told her mother and they got help through a local NACADA counsellor before his addiction became severe.
🛡️ How to Avoid Drugs — Practical Strategies

Staying drug-free requires more than just knowing drugs are harmful. It requires building a strong support system and practical refusal skills.

🔑 Peer Pressure: Direct or indirect influence from friends or social groups to behave in a certain way — including trying drugs. You have the RIGHT to say no.

💬 How to Help Someone Who is Using Drugs

If you suspect a friend or family member is using drugs, approach with care and empathy — not judgment.

  • Choose a calm, private moment to talk
  • Use "I" statements: "I'm worried about you because I've noticed..."
  • Listen without interrupting or blaming
  • Suggest professional help — NACADA helpline: 1192
  • Do NOT give ultimatums or threaten them publicly
  • Take care of your own mental health too — it is stressful supporting someone with addiction
🇰🇪 Fatuma, Mombasa: Her classmate Rashid started selling bhang to fund his own habit. Rather than reporting him to the teacher (which she feared would get him expelled), Fatuma spoke privately with the school counsellor who arranged an intervention. Rashid is now in Form 4 and clean.
