🛡️ What is Abstinence?

Abstinence means choosing not to engage in sexual activity. It is the only 100% effective method to prevent both sexually transmitted infections (STIs) and unplanned pregnancy.

🔑 Sexual Abstinence: A deliberate personal decision to refrain from sexual intercourse — for health, moral, religious, or personal reasons. It is a right and a valid choice.

💪 Handling Pressure to Have Sex

Many teenagers face direct or indirect pressure — from partners, peers, or media. Knowing how to respond confidently protects you.

  • "Everyone is doing it" — They are NOT. Research shows many teens overestimate how sexually active their peers are.
  • "If you loved me, you would" — Love does not demand anything that makes you uncomfortable. This is manipulation.
  • "It's just once" — Pregnancy and STIs can happen from one encounter.
  • "You'll seem childish" — Maturity is knowing your values and standing by them.
⚠️ Legal Note: In Kenya, sexual activity with anyone under 18 is a criminal offence — regardless of consent. Older partners who pressure you are committing a crime.
