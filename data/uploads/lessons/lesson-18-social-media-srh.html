📱 Social Media & Sexual Health

Social media platforms — Facebook, TikTok, Instagram, WhatsApp, Snapchat — have transformed how young Kenyans communicate, date, and access information. But they also carry serious SRH risks.

Platform RiskSRH Impact
Explicit contentNormalises unrealistic and harmful sexual expectations
Online dating appsCasual hookup culture, increased STI risk
AnonymityDeception about age, identity, and intentions
Peer pressure via likesPressure to post sexual content to gain validation
Cybersex/sextingContent can be screenshot and shared without consent
🇰🇪 Aisha, Mombasa (16): Befriended a "25-year-old student" on Instagram. They exchanged messages for weeks. He asked her to share intimate photos, promising he would delete them. She did. He shared them in a WhatsApp group at her school. She was devastated, missed school for 2 weeks, and needed counselling. The man was 38. This is image-based abuse and a criminal offence in Kenya.
🚫 Cyberbullying & Its Effects

Cyberbullying uses digital technology to harass, threaten, or humiliate someone. In Kenya, it is increasingly common on WhatsApp, TikTok, and school group chats.

TypeExample
HarassmentRepeated threatening or insulting messages
DoxingSharing someone's private information publicly
ImpersonationCreating a fake account using someone's photos
Sexting image sharingSharing intimate photos without consent
TrollingPosting inflammatory comments to provoke
⚠️ Effects of Cyberbullying: Depression, anxiety, school refusal, suicidal thoughts, and social isolation. Cyberbullying is a crime in Kenya under the Computer Misuse and Cybercrimes Act 2018.
🔐 Staying Safe Online — 8 Rules
  • Never share intimate photos — once sent, you lose control forever
  • Never share your location, home address, or school details with strangers
  • Use strong, unique passwords and enable 2-factor authentication
  • Set all accounts to private; vet friend/follower requests carefully
  • Block and report abusive users immediately; document evidence
  • Meet online friends only in public places with a trusted adult
  • If something makes you uncomfortable online, tell a trusted adult
  • Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities
