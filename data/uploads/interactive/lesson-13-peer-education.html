⭐ Skills of a Peer EducatorUjuzi wa Mwelimishaji wa RikaSkills za Peer Educator

Good peer educators are made, not born. These skills can all be learnt and practised.Waelimishaji wazuri wa rika huundwa, si kuzaliwa. Ujuzi huu wote unaweza kufunzwa na kufanyiwa mazoezi.Good peer educators huundwa, si kuzaliwa. Skills hizi zote zinaweza kufunzwa na ku-practise.

  • Accurate knowledge — know your facts before sharingUjuzi sahihi — jua ukweli wako kabla ya kushirikiAccurate knowledge — jua facts zako kabla ya kushiriki
  • Active listening — hear people out fully before respondingKusikiliza kwa makini — sikiliza kikamilifu kabla ya kujibuActive listening — sikiliza kikamilifu kabla ya ku-respond
  • Non-judgmental attitude — create a safe space for allMtazamo usio na hukumu — unda nafasi salama kwa woteNon-judgmental attitude — unda safe space kwa wote
  • Confidentiality — what is shared stays private (safety exceptions)Usiri — kilichoshirikiwa kinabaki siri (isipokuwa usalama)Confidentiality — kinachoshirikiwa kinabaki siri (safety exceptions)
  • Referral — know when to connect someone to professional helpRufaa — jua wakati wa kumwunganisha mtu na msaada wa kitaalamuReferral — jua wakati wa kumconnect mtu na professional help
  • Modelling — live the values you promoteKuiga — ishi maadili unayohimizaModelling — live values unazopromote
⚠️ Limits of Peer Education⚠️ Mipaka ya Elimu ya Rika⚠️ Limits za Peer Education Peer educators are NOT counsellors or doctors. When a peer needs medical help, is in danger, or discloses abuse — ALWAYS refer to a trusted adult or professional. Waelimishaji wa rika si washauri wala madaktari. Mrika anapohitaji msaada wa kimatibabu, yuko hatarini, au anafichua unyanyasaji — DAIMA mpeleke kwa mtu mzima anayeaminiwa au mtaalamu. Peer educators si counsellors wala madaktari. Mrika anapohitaji medical help, yuko hatarini, au anafichua abuse — DAIMA mrefer kwa trusted adult au professional.

🌱 Spreading Positive InfluenceKusambaza Ushawishi MzuriSpreading Positive Influence

One peer educator who reaches 10 peers — and those 10 each reach 10 more — creates a ripple of 100 changed lives.Mwelimishaji mmoja wa rika anayefikia rika 10 — na kila mmoja wa 10 anafikia 10 zaidi — huunda mawimbi ya maisha 100 yaliyobadilishwa.Peer educator mmoja anayefikia peers 10 — na kila mmoja wa 10 anafikia 10 zaidi — huunda ripple ya maisha 100 yaliyobadilishwa.

  • Share accurate SRH information in your friendship groupShiriki habari sahihi za SRH katika kikundi chako cha urafikiShiriki accurate SRH information kwa friendship group yako
  • Challenge myths and misinformation gently but firmlyPinga hadithi za uongo na habari potofu kwa upole lakini kwa nguvuChallenge myths na misinformation kwa upole lakini kwa nguvu
  • Model healthy behaviour — your actions speak louder than wordsOnyesha tabia yenye afya — matendo yako yanazungumza zaidi kuliko manenoModel healthy behaviour — actions zako zinazungumza louder kuliko maneno
  • Connect peers in need to trusted adults and servicesUnganisha rika wanaohitaji na watu wazima wanaoaminiwa na hudumaConnect peers wanaohitaji na trusted adults na services
  • Create safe spaces for honest conversationsUnda nafasi salama kwa mazungumzo ya uaminifuUnda safe spaces kwa honest conversations
