⚠️ Cortisol Warning⚠️ Onyo la Cortisol⚠️ Cortisol Warning Cortisol (the stress hormone) is helpful in short bursts but destructive when chronically elevated. It literally shrinks the hippocampus (memory centre of the brain), impairs immune function, disrupts sleep, and triggers depression. Managing stress is not optional — it is biological necessity. Cortisol (homoni ya msongo) ni ya manufaa kwa muda mfupi lakini inadhuru ikiwa imeinuliwa kwa muda mrefu. Kweli inashrink hippocampus (kituo cha kumbukumbu cha ubongo), inadhuru mfumo wa kinga, inakera usingizi, na huchochea unyogovu. Kusimamia msongo si hiari — ni haja ya kibiolojia.
