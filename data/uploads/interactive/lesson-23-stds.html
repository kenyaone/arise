🦠 STDs — The BasicsMisingi ya STISTDs — The Basics

Sexually Transmitted Diseases (STDs) are among the most common infections in Kenya, but most are preventable and many are curable. The key is knowledge, testing, and early treatment. Magonjwa ya zinaa (STI) ni miongoni mwa maambukizi ya kawaida zaidi Kenya, lakini mengi yanazuiwa na mengi yanaposwa. Ufunguo ni elimu, kupima, na matibabu ya mapema. Magonjwa ya zinaa (STDs) ni miongoni mwa common infections Kenya, lakini mengi yanazuiwa na mengi yanaweza kuposwa. Ufunguo ni knowledge, kupima, na early treatment.

🔑 STD / STI🔑 Ugonjwa wa Zinaa🔑 STD / STI Sexually Transmitted Disease/Infection — infections spread primarily through sexual contact. Kenya reports 3+ million STI cases per year. Many show NO symptoms. Ugonjwa/Maambukizi ya Zinaa — maambukizi yanayosambazwa hasa kupitia ngono. Kenya inaona visa 3+ milioni vya STI kwa mwaka. Mengi hayana dalili. 🔴 Gonorrhoea & Chlamydia — The Silent EpidemicsKisonono na Klamidia — Magonjwa ya KimyaGonorrhoea & Chlamydia — The Silent Epidemics

Gonorrhoea and chlamydia are the most common bacterial STDs in Kenya. They often cause NO symptoms — especially in women — making them dangerous if left untreated. Kisonono na klamidia ni STI za bakteria zinazojulikana zaidi Kenya. Mara nyingi hazina dalili — hasa kwa wanawake — na hii ni hatari bila matibabu. Gonorrhoea na chlamydia ni most common bacterial STDs Kenya. Mara nyingi hazina symptoms — hasa kwa wanawake — na hii ni hatari bila treatment.

🔑 Gonorrhoea (Kisonono)🔑 Kisonono🔑 Gonorrhoea (Kisonono) Caused by Neisseria gonorrhoeae. Symptoms: burning urination, yellowish discharge, pelvic pain. Often symptomless in women. Can cause infertility if untreated. Curable with antibiotics. Husababishwa na Neisseria gonorrhoeae. Dalili: maumivu wakati wa kukojoa, kutoka usaha wa njano, maumivu ya tumbo la chini. 🟡 Herpes & HPV — Viral STDsHerpes na HPV — Magonjwa ya VirusiHerpes & HPV — Viral STDs

Viral STDs cannot be cured but can be managed with medication and prevented through vaccination and protective behaviours. Magonjwa ya zinaa ya virusi hayawezi kuposwa lakini yanaweza kudhibitiwa kwa dawa na kuzuiwa kwa chanjo na tabia za kinga. Viral STDs haziwezi curiwa lakini zinaweza managiwa na medication na kuzuiwa kwa vaccination na protective behaviours.
