🚫 What Is FGM?FGM ni Nini?FGM ni Nini?

FGM is the partial or total removal or injury of the external female genital organs for non-medical reasons. It has NO health benefits and causes lifelong harm.FGM ni kuondolewa kwa sehemu au zote au kuumiza sehemu za nje za siri za kike kwa sababu zisizo za kimatibabu. Haina faida ya kiafya na husababisha madhara ya maisha yote.FGM ni kuondolewa kwa sehemu au zote au kuumiza external female genitalia kwa non-medical reasons. Haina health benefits na husababisha lifelong harm.

⚖️ Legal Framework & ReportingMfumo wa Kisheria na KuripotiLegal Framework & Reporting

FGM is a crime in Kenya. Knowing the law protects girls.FGM ni kosa la jinai Kenya. Kujua sheria kunalinda wasichana.FGM ni crime Kenya. Kujua law kunalinda wasichana.

🔑 Prohibition of FGM Act 2011🔑 Sheria ya Kupiga Marufuku FGM 2011🔑 Prohibition of FGM Act 2011Criminalises performing FGM, aiding or abetting FGM, taking a girl across borders for FGM, and failing to report at-risk girls. Maximum 3 years imprisonment or KES 200,000 fine, or both.Inafanya FGM, kusaidia FGM, kupeleka msichana ng'ambo kwa FGM, na kutokuripoti wasichana walio hatarini, kuwa makosa ya jinai.
