🚫 What Is FGM?FGM ni Nini?FGM ni Nini?

FGM is the partial or total removal or injury of the external female genital organs for non-medical reasons. It has NO health benefits and causes lifelong harm.FGM ni kuondolewa kwa sehemu au zote au kuumiza sehemu za nje za siri za kike kwa sababu zisizo za kimatibabu. Haina faida ya kiafya na husababisha madhara ya maisha yote.FGM ni kuondolewa kwa sehemu au zote au kuumiza external female genitalia kwa non-medical reasons. Haina health benefits na husababisha lifelong harm.
