🌿 What is Cannabis? The Street Names & the Science Cannabis ni Nini? Majina ya Mtaani na Sayansi Bangi ni Nini? Street Names na Science

Cannabis sativa is a plant containing over 100 active compounds called cannabinoids. It is known by many names across Kenya. Cannabis sativa ni mmea wenye zaidi ya kemikali 100 zinazoitwa cannabinoids. Inajulikana kwa majina mengi kote Kenya. Cannabis sativa ni mmea ina chemicals zaidi ya 100 zinazoitwa cannabinoids. 🔑 THC (Tetrahydrocannabinol) — The Psychoactive Compound 🔑 THC — Kemikali Inayobadilisha Akili 🔑 THC — Kitu Kinachodunda Ubongo THC is the chemical in cannabis that causes the "high" — altered perception, euphoria, and paranoia. It binds to cannabinoid receptors in the brain, particularly in areas controlling memory, coordination, and reward. THC ni kemikali katika bangi inayosababisha "high" — mabadiliko ya mtazamo, furaha ya bandia, na wasiwasi. Inaunganika na vipokezi vya cannabinoid kwenye ubongo, hasa maeneo yanayodhibiti kumbukumbu na malipo. THC ndiyo kitu kinachokufanya "fly" — ubongo inabadilika, unaona vitu tofauti, unafeel high. Inakaa kwa receptors za ubongo — hasa sehemu ya kumbukumbu na reward.
🔑 CBD (Cannabidiol) — The Non-Psychoactive Compound 🔑 CBD — Kemikali Isiyobadilisha Akili 🔑 CBD — Hii Haikudund Ubongo CBD does NOT cause a high. It has limited medical uses (e.g. certain epilepsy conditions) under strict prescription — but ONLY in pharmaceutical-grade form, not street cannabis which contains dangerous levels of THC. CBD HAISABABISHI high. Ina matumizi kidogo ya kimatibabu chini ya dawa — lakini si kwa bangi ya mtaani ambayo ina THC nyingi za hatari. CBD haikufanya high. Ina uses kidogo za dawa — lakini bangi ya mtaani si CBD. Bangi ya street ina THC mob sana.
⚠️ Illegal in Kenya: ⚠️ Haramu nchini Kenya: ⚠️ Haramu Kenya: Cannabis is a Class B controlled substance under Kenya's Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act Cap 245. Possession can lead to arrest and imprisonment. There are no legal cannabis dispensaries in Kenya. Bangi ni dawa iliyopigwa marufuku chini ya Sheria ya Dawa za Kulevya Cap 245. 🧠 Why Teens Are in More Danger Than Adults Kwa Nini Vijana Wako Katika Hatari Zaidi Kwa Nini Sisi Tuko Hatarini Zaidi Kuliko Wazee

This is the most important fact in this entire lesson: your brain is not fully developed until age 25. Cannabis permanently damages a brain that is still developing in ways it does NOT damage a mature adult brain. Hii ndiyo ukweli muhimu zaidi katika somo hili lote: ubongo wako haujakamilika hadi umri wa miaka 25. Bangi inaharibu ubongo unaokua kwa njia ambayo haiharibu ubongo wa mtu mzima. Hii ndiyo the most important thing katika lesson yote: ubongo yako haijafika 100% hadi una miaka 25. Bangi inaharibu ubongo inayokua kwa njia tofauti kabisa na mzee.

⚠️ The Prefrontal Cortex — What's Still Being Built ⚠️ Sehemu ya Ubongo Inayoendelea Kukua ⚠️ Sehemu ya Ubongo Bado Inabuildiwa
The prefrontal cortex controls: judgment, decision-making, impulse control, memory, and emotional regulation. THC disrupts the development of this exact region. The damage is permanent — the brain cannot repair it later. Prefrontal cortex inadhibiti: hukumu, kufanya maamuzi, kudhibiti msukumo, kumbukumbu, na udhibiti wa hisia. THC inazuia ukuaji wa sehemu hii hasa. Uharibifu ni wa kudumu — ubongo hauwezi kujitengeneza baadaye. Prefrontal cortex inadhibiti: maamuzi, kumbukumbu, kudhibiti anger yako, emotions. THC inakatakata development ya sehemu hii. Na damage ni ya maisha yote — hailipiki.

🔴 Cannabis and Mental Health — The Hidden Risk Bangi na Afya ya Akili — Hatari Iliyofichwa Bangi na Mental Health — Hatari Ambayo Watu Hawasemi

Most people only think about the physical effects of cannabis. The mental health risks are less talked about — but for some people, they are catastrophic. Watu wengi wanafikiri tu kuhusu athari za kimwili za bangi. Hatari za afya ya akili hazizungumzwi sana — lakini kwa baadhi ya watu, zinaweza kuwa mbaya kabisa. Watu wanazungumza macho mekundu na kumbukumbu mbaya. Lakini hakuna anayekuambia kuhusu mental health — na hii ndiyo hatari kubwa zaidi.

🔑 Cannabis-Induced Psychosis: 🔑 Psychosis Inayosababishwa na Bangi: 🔑 Bangi-Induced Madness: A serious mental health condition triggered by cannabis use — symptoms include hearing voices, paranoid delusions (strong false beliefs), disorganised thinking, and loss of contact with reality. Can occur after just a few uses in genetically vulnerable individuals. Can become permanent schizophrenia. Hali mbaya ya afya ya akili inayosababishwa na bangi — dalili ni kusikia sauti, imani za uongo, mawazo yasiyopangwa, na kupoteza uhalisia. Inaweza kutokea baada ya matumizi machache tu kwa watu wanaohusika kwa urithi. Inaweza kuwa schizophrenia ya kudumu. Ugonjwa wa akili unaosababishwa na bangi — unasikia voices, unadhani mambo ya uongo, mawazo yako yanapotea. Inaweza kutokea kwa mtu baada ya ku-smoke mara chache tu — kama DNA yake iko hivyo. Inaweza kuwa madness ya maisha yote.
⚠️ You cannot know in advance if you are genetically vulnerable. ⚠️ Huwezi kujua mapema kama uko katika hatari ya urithi. ⚠️ Huwezi kujua kama uko kwa hatari — hadi inakupata. Family history of mental illness increases risk significantly. But psychosis can also occur in people with no family history. The risk is real and it is irreversible. Historia ya familia ya ugonjwa wa akili huongeza hatari sana. Lakini psychosis inaweza pia kutokea kwa watu wasio na historia ya familia. Hatari ni ya kweli na haiwezi kugeuzwa. Kama family yako ina history ya mental illness, uko hatarini zaidi. Lakini hata kama hawana — inaweza ikupata. Na hailipiki.
