🧠 🧠 Brain
Cathinone in fresh miraa triggers dopamine and norepinephrine release — similar to amphetamines. Creates euphoria then crash. Chronic use leads to dependence, and heavy long-term use raises the risk of psychosis and depression.
❤️ ❤️ Heart & Circulation
Miraa is a stimulant — it raises heart rate and blood pressure immediately. Long-term use increases risk of myocardial infarction (heart attack) and contributes to hypertension, especially dangerous when combined with caffeine.
🦷 🦷 Teeth, Gums & Jaw
Chewing miraa causes green-brown staining, gum recession, tooth decay and loose teeth. The constant jaw movement causes temporomandibular joint (TMJ) pain. Mouth cancer risk rises with years of use.
🟤 🟤 Stomach & Gut
Miraa suppresses appetite, causing malnutrition in heavy users. It causes constipation, gastritis, and is linked to liver inflammation. The crash after a session often causes nausea and cramping.
🔵 🔵 Skin & Appearance
Chronic users develop a gaunt, malnourished appearance — sunken cheeks, weight loss, sallow skin. Insomnia from the stimulant effect creates dark circles and chronic fatigue. Social and economic deterioration follows.
🟣 🟣 Reproductive System
Miraa lowers libido and is linked to reduced sperm quality and count in men. In women it disrupts the menstrual cycle. Children of heavy miraa users face increased risk of low birth weight.

Scenario: Brian, 17, is in a social group where miraa is being chewed. An older person says: "Chew this — it'll make you confident and energetic. There are girls here who like a confident man. Don't sit in the corner."
What should Brian do?
Someone laughs: "You're scared of a plant? Real men don't hesitate." How does Brian respond?
✅
Confident Without a Substance — Best Outcome

Brian demonstrated real confidence — by exercising his own judgment under pressure. Miraa (khat) contains cathinone, which creates euphoria and lowers inhibition. Lowered inhibition in a social setting with "interested girls" is a designed risk environment. Brian's refusal was the confident choice.

⚠️
Safe Without Engaging

Moving away without arguing was smart. The group testing him was looking for a reaction — not getting one deflated the pressure.

An older girl sits next to Brian: "You're interesting. Come with me." What now?
✅
Held the Boundary Without the Substance

Brian was in the social environment but not impaired. He could assess the situation clearly and decline. Clear judgment is your best protection in any social situation.

❌
Social Pressure Led to Isolation

Being led away by an older person while others use substances is a risk scenario for any gender. Young men who cause unintended pregnancies face consequences too: financial, legal, and emotional. Being unimpaired and assertive protects everyone.

❌
Substance + Social Engineering

The framing — "confidence + interested girls" — was designed to connect the substance with a sexual reward. Miraa's cathinone creates euphoria that lowers inhibitions and impairs judgment. Real confidence does not come from a plant — it comes from knowing your own values.
