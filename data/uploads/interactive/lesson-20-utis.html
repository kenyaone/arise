🛡️ Prevention — Daily HabitsKinga — Tabia za Kila SikuPrevention — Daily Habits

Most UTIs are preventable with simple, free daily habits. Start today.UTI nyingi zinazuiwa na tabia rahisi, za bure za kila siku. Anza leo.UTI nyingi zinazuiwa na simple, free daily habits. Anza leo.

  • 💧 Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily — flushes bacteria from the urinary tract💧 Kunywa angalau glasi 8 za maji kila siku — hufusha bakteria kutoka njia ya mkojo💧 Kunywa angalau glasi 8 za maji kila siku — huflusha bacteria kutoka urinary tract
  • 🚽 Urinate regularly — do not hold urine for long periods🚽 Kojoa mara kwa mara — usishikilie mkojo kwa muda mrefu🚽 Kojoa mara kwa mara — usishikilie urine kwa muda mrefu
  • 🧻 Wipe front to back ALWAYS after using the toilet (girls)🧻 Futa mbele kwenda nyuma DAIMA baada ya kutumia choo (wasichana)🧻 Futa front to back DAIMA baada ya kutumia choo (wasichana)
  • 👙 Wear cotton underwear — reduces moisture that promotes bacterial growth👙 Vaa chupi ya pamba — hupunguza unyevu unaokuza ukuaji wa bakteria👙 Vaa cotton underwear — hupunguza moisture inayokuza bacterial growth
  • 🩸 Change pads/tampons every 4–6 hours during menstruation🩸 Badilisha pedi/tamponi kila masaa 4–6 wakati wa hedhi🩸 Badilisha pads/tampons kila hours 4–6 wakati wa menstruation
