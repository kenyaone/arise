❤️ Healthy vs Unhealthy RelationshipsMahusiano ya Afya dhidi ya YasiyofaaHealthy vs Unhealthy Relationships
🔑 Boundary🔑 Mpaka🔑 Boundary A clear limit that defines what behaviour you will and will not accept from others. Kikomo wazi kinachoelezea tabia utakayokubali na usiokubali kutoka kwa wengine. Clear limit inayodefine tabia utakayoaccept na hautaaccept kutoka kwa wengine.

Healthy relationships have: mutual respect, trust, honesty, shared decision-making, and space for individuality. Mahusiano ya afya yana: heshima ya pande zote, uaminifu, uwazi, maamuzi ya pamoja, na nafasi ya utu binafsi. Healthy relationships zina: mutual respect, trust, honesty, shared decision-making, na nafasi ya individuality.

Unhealthy relationships show: jealousy disguised as care, pressure, control, disrespect, and exclusion of other friendships. Mahusiano yasiyofaa yanaonyesha: wivu uliofichwa kama huduma, shinikizo, udhibiti, kukosa heshima, na kuwatenga marafiki wengine. Unhealthy relationships zinaonyesha: jealousy iliyofichwa kama care, pressure, control, disrespect, na ku-exclude marafiki wengine.

⚠ Warning:⚠ Onyo:⚠ Warning: An unhealthy friendship or romantic relationship can cause depression, a drop in grades, risky behaviour, and isolation from your support network. Urafiki au mahusiano ya kimapenzi yasiyofaa yanaweza kusababisha unyogovu, kushuka kwa daraja, tabia hatari, na kutengwa na mtandao wako wa msaada. Unhealthy friendship au romantic relationship inaweza kusababisha depression, kushuka kwa grades, risky behaviour, na isolation kutoka kwa support network yako.

🔄 Pressure in Relationships — Common Scenarios in KenyaShinikizo katika Mahusiano — Hali Zinazojitokeza KenyaPressure katika Relationships — Common Scenarios Kenya

Learn to recognise these patterns before they trap you:Jifunze kutambua mifumo hii kabla haijakukamata:Jifunza kutambua patterns hizi kabla hazijaku-trap:

1. “If you don’t come to my house today, you don’t love me” — Emotional Blackmail“Usipokuja nyumbani kwangu leo, hunipenzi” — Vitisho vya Kihisia“Usipokuja kwangu leo, hunipenzi” — Emotional Blackmail Using love as a condition to force compliance. Real love does not come with ultimatums. Kutumia upendo kama sharti la kulazimisha utiifu. Upendo wa kweli hauja na vitisho. Kutumia love kama condition ku-force compliance. Real love haji na ultimatums.
2. “Stop talking to your other friends — you only need me” — Isolation“Acha kuzungumza na marafiki zako wengine — unahitaji mimi tu” — Kutengwa“Acha kuongea na marafiki wako wengine — unahitaji mimi tu” — Isolation Cutting you off from support networks is a key warning sign of coercive control. Kukukatia kutoka kwa mitandao ya msaada ni ishara muhimu ya onyo ya udhibiti wa kulazimisha. Kukukata kutoka kwa support networks ni key warning sign ya coercive control.
3. “Everyone in our relationship group does it — why are you different?” — Peer Normalising“Kila mtu katika kikundi chetu anafanya — kwa nini wewe ni tofauti?” — Kawaida ya Rika“Kila mtu kwa group yetu anafanya — kwa nini wewe ni different?” — Peer Normalising Claiming harmful behaviour is normal to reduce your resistance. It is not normal — it is pressure. Kudai tabia hatari ni ya kawaida kupunguza upinzani wako. Si ya kawaida — ni shinikizo. Kudai harmful behaviour ni normal kupunguza resistance yako. Si normal — ni pressure.
4. “You owe me — I’ve done so much for you” — Entitlement / Debt Framing“Unanidaiwa — nimefanya mengi sana kwa ajili yako” — Fremu ya Deni“Unanidaiwa — nimefanya mengi sana kwa ajili yako” — Debt Framing Gifts or favours do not create an obligation to comply with harmful demands. You owe no one your body or safety. Zawadi au upendeleo haulazimishi kukubaliana na mahitaji yenye madhara. Huna deni mwili wako au usalama wako kwa mtu yeyote. Gifts au favours hazikulazimishi kukubaliana na harmful demands. Huna-deni mwili wako au safety yako kwa mtu yeyote.
5. “Share your location with me at all times” — Digital Control“Nishirikishe eneo lako wakati wote” — Udhibiti wa Kidijitali“Share location yako nami wakati wote” — Digital Control Demanding constant access to your phone, location, or passwords is surveillance, not love. Kudai upatikanaji wa kudumu wa simu yako, eneo, au nywila ni ufuatiliaji, si upendo. Kudai constant access ya phone yako, location, au passwords ni surveillance, si love.
💡 Remember:💡 Kumbuka:💡 Remember: Any relationship that makes you feel guilty for having boundaries is not healthy. Mahusiano yoyote yanayokufanya uhisi hatia kwa kuwa na mipaka si ya afya. Relationship yoyote inayokufanya uhisi guilty kwa kuwa na boundaries si healthy.

🚫 Ending Unhealthy Relationships SafelyKumalizia Mahusiano Yasiyofaa kwa UsalamaKumaliza Unhealthy Relationships kwa Usalama

  • Do it in a public place or with a trusted person nearbyFanya katika mahali pa watu au na mtu wa kuamini karibuFanya mahali pa watu au na trusted person karibu
  • Be clear and final: “I am ending this relationship. Please do not contact me again.”Kuwa wazi na wa mwisho: “Ninamaliza mahusiano haya. Tafadhali usiniwasiliane tena.”Kuwa clear na final: “Ninamaliza relationship hii. Tafadhali usiwasiliane nami tena.”
  • Block on all platforms if neededZuia kwenye majukwaa yote ikiwa inahitajikaBlock kwa platforms zote kama inahitajika
  • If they threaten you: screenshot evidence, report to a trusted adult, Childline 116, or policeWakikutishia: piga picha ya ushahidi, ripoti kwa mtu mzima wa kuamini, Childline 116, au polisiWakikutishia: screenshot evidence, ripoti kwa trusted adult, Childline 116, au polisi
