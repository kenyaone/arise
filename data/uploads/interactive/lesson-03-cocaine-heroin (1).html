🔑 How Cocaine Works🔑 Jinsi Kokeni Inavyofanya Kazi🔑 Jinsi Cocaine Inavyofanya KaziCocaine blocks dopamine reuptake — dopamine floods the brain causing intense but short euphoria. Receptors burn out fast. Without cocaine the brain cannot generate normal pleasure, causing deep depression and intense craving.Kokeni inazuia kurudishwa kwa dopamine — dopamine inajaza ubongo ikisababisha furaha kali lakini ya muda mfupi. Vipokezi vinachoka haraka. Bila kokeni ubongo hauwezi kuzalisha raha ya kawaida, na kusababisha unyogovu na kutamani kwa nguvu.Cocaine inazuia dopamine reuptake — dopamine inajaza ubongo ikisababisha intense euphoria ya muda mfupi. Receptors zinachoka haraka. Bila cocaine ubongo hauwezi kuzalisha normal pleasure, ikisababisha deep depression na intense craving.

💉 Heroin — The Physical TrapHeroini — Mtego wa KimwiliHeroin — Mtego wa Kimwili

Heroin creates one of the most brutal physical addictions known to medicine — and one of the most dangerous to life.Heroini huunda mojawapo ya uraibu wa kimwili wa kutisha zaidi unaojulikana na dawa — na mojawapo ya hatari zaidi kwa maisha.Heroin huunda mojawapo ya most brutal physical addictions inayojulikana na medicine — na mojawapo ya most dangerous.

🔑 How Heroin Works🔑 Jinsi Heroini Inavyofanya Kazi🔑 Jinsi Heroin Inavyofanya KaziHeroin converts to morphine in the brain and binds to opioid receptors — intense pain relief and euphoria. Physical dependence develops in days. Without it: brutal withdrawal. Overdose stops breathing.Heroini inabadilika kuwa morphine kwenye ubongo na inaunganika na vipokezi vya opioid — maumivu makali kupungua na furaha. Utegemezi wa kimwili hukua kwa siku chache. Bila hiyo: kujiepusha kwa nguvu. Overdose inasimamisha kupumua.Heroin inabadilika kuwa morphine kwenye ubongo na inaunganika na opioid receptors — intense pain relief na euphoria. Physical dependence hukua kwa siku chache. Bila hiyo: brutal withdrawal. Overdose inasimamisha kupumua.
