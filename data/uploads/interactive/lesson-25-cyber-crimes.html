🖥 What Are Cyber Crimes?Uhalifu wa Mtandao ni Nini?Cyber Crimes ni Nini?

Cyber crimes are criminal activities carried out using computers, the internet, or digital devices. They are taken very seriously under Kenyan law. Uhalifu wa mtandao ni shughuli za uhalifu zinazofanywa kwa kutumia kompyuta, intaneti, au vifaa vya kidijitali. Zinaangaliwa kwa uzito chini ya sheria ya Kenya. Cyber crimes ni criminal activities zinazofanywa kwa kutumia computers, internet, au digital devices. Zinachukuliwa seriously sana chini ya sheria ya Kenya.

🔑 Computer Misuse and Cybercrimes Act 2018 (CMCA)🔑 Sheria ya Matumizi Mabaya ya Kompyuta 2018🔑 Computer Misuse and Cybercrimes Act 2018 (CMCA) Kenya's comprehensive law criminalising online offences — passed in 2018. Covers hacking, cyberbullying, sextortion, identity theft, online fraud, and more. Penalties range from fines to decades in prison. Sheria ya Kenya inayoshughulikia makosa ya mtandaoni — ilipitishwa 2018. Inashughulikia udukuzi, uonevu wa mtandao, sextortion, wizi wa utambulisho, udanganyifu wa mtandao na zaidi. Adhabu zinatoka faini hadi miaka mingi gerezani. Kenya's comprehensive law inayocriminalise online offences — ilipitishwa 2018. Inashughulikia hacking, cyberbullying, sextortion, identity theft, online fraud, na zaidi. Penalties zinatoka fines hadi decades gerezani.

💰 Online Fraud & ScamsUlaghai na Udanganyifu wa MtandaoOnline Fraud & Scams

Online fraud is one of the most common cyber crimes facing young Kenyans. Scammers use fake messages, calls, and websites to steal money and personal information. Ulaghai wa mtandao ni mojawapo ya uhalifu wa kawaida zaidi unaowakabili vijana wa Kenya. Walaghai hutumia ujumbe bandia, simu, na tovuti kuiba pesa na taarifa za kibinafsi. Online fraud ni moja ya most common cyber crimes inayowakabili vijana wa Kenya. Scammers hutumia fake messages, calls, na websites kuiba pesa na personal info.

  • M-Pesa fraud: criminals pose as Safaricom or banks asking for your PIN or OTP codesUlaghai wa M-Pesa: wahalifu wanajifanya Safaricom au benki wakiomba PIN au nambari za OTPM-Pesa fraud: criminals wanajifanya Safaricom au banks wakiomba PIN yako au OTP codes
  • Fake jobs & scholarships: posts promising jobs or study funds that require you to "pay to register"Kazi na usomi bandia: machapisho yanayoahidi kazi au fedha za masomo yanayohitaji "kulipa kujiandikisha"Fake jobs & scholarships: posts zinazoahidi kazi au scholarship zinazohitaji "kulipa kuregister"
  • Investment scams ("double your money"): promises of impossible returns — always a scamUdanganyifu wa uwekezaji ("mara mbili pesa yako"): ahadi za faida zisizowezekana — ni ulaghai kila wakatiInvestment scams ("double your money"): promises za impossible returns — ni scam kila wakati
  • SIM swap fraud: criminals take over your mobile number to access your M-Pesa and bank accountsUlaghai wa kubadilisha SIM: wahalifu wanachukua nambari yako ya simu kupata M-Pesa na akaunti za benkiSIM swap fraud: criminals wanachukua mobile number yako kupata M-Pesa na bank accounts zako
🔑 Phishing🔑 Uvuvi wa Taarifa (Phishing)🔑 Phishing A fraudulent attempt to obtain sensitive information by disguising as a trustworthy entity — fake Safaricom SMS, fake bank emails, fake prize websites. Jaribio la udanganyifu la kupata taarifa nyeti kwa kujifanya chombo kinachoweza kuaminiwa — ujumbe wa Safaricom bandia, barua pepe za benki bandia, tovuti za tuzo bandia. Fraudulent attempt ya kupata sensitive info kwa kujifanya trustworthy entity — fake Safaricom SMS, fake bank emails, fake prize websites.

😥 CyberbullyingUonevu wa MtandaoCyberbullying

Cyberbullying is using digital technology to repeatedly harm or harass another person. It can happen on WhatsApp, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook — anywhere online. Uonevu wa mtandao ni kutumia teknolojia ya kidijitali kudhuru au kunyanyasa mtu mwingine mara kwa mara. Unaweza kutokea kwenye WhatsApp, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook — popote mtandaoni. Cyberbullying ni kutumia digital technology repeatedly kuharass mtu mwingine. Inaweza happen kwenye WhatsApp, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook — popote online.

  • Sending threatening, humiliating, or hateful messages onlineKutuma ujumbe wa vitisho, udhalilishaji, au chuki mtandaoniKutuma threatening, humiliating, au hateful messages online
  • Posting rumours, fake content, or private information about someoneKuchapisha uvumi, maudhui ya uongo, au taarifa za kibinafsi za mtuKupost rumours, fake content, au private info ya mtu
  • Creating fake profiles to impersonate and harass othersKuunda wasifu bandia kuiga na kunyanyasa wengineKuunda fake profiles kujifanya na kuharass wengine
  • Deliberately excluding someone from online groups to isolate themKumtenga mtu kimakusudi kutoka vikundi vya mtandao kumtengaDeliberately kumexclude mtu kutoka online groups kumtenga
⚠️ Real Consequences⚠️ Matokeo Halisi⚠️ Real Consequences Victims of cyberbullying experience depression, anxiety, school absenteeism, social isolation, and in extreme cases suicidal ideation. 30% of Kenyan youth report being cyberbullied. Wahanga wa uonevu wa mtandao wapata msongo wa mawazo, wasiwasi, kukimbia shule, kutengwa kijamii, na katika hali mbaya mawazo ya kujiua. 30% ya vijana wa Kenya wanaripoti kunyang'anywa mtandaoni. Victims wa cyberbullying wapata depression, anxiety, school absenteeism, social isolation, na extreme cases suicidal ideation. 30% ya Kenyan youth wanaripoti cyberbullying.
🔑 Legal Status — CMCA 2018, Section 27🔑 Hali ya Kisheria — CMCA 2018, Kifungu 27🔑 Legal Status — CMCA 2018, Section 27 Cyberbullying is a criminal offence in Kenya. Conviction carries up to 2 years imprisonment or a fine of KES 200,000 — or both. Uonevu wa mtandao ni kosa la jinai Kenya. Hukumu inabeba hadi miaka 2 gerezani au faini ya KES 200,000 — au vyote viwili. Cyberbullying ni criminal offence Kenya. Conviction ina hadi miaka 2 gerezani au fine ya KES 200,000 — au vyote viwili.

🛑 Sextortion & Non-Consensual Image SharingSextortion na Kushiriki Picha Bila RidhaaSextortion & Non-Consensual Sharing

These are among the most serious and traumatic cyber crimes. They disproportionately target young people and often cause severe psychological harm. Hizi ni miongoni mwa uhalifu mbaya na wa kiwewe zaidi wa mtandao. Zinawalenga vijana zaidi na mara nyingi husababisha madhara makubwa ya kisaikolojia. Hizi ni miongoni mwa serious na traumatic cyber crimes. Zinalenga vijana zaidi na mara nyingi husababisha severe psychological harm.

🔑 Sextortion🔑 Sextortion🔑 Sextortion Threatening to share intimate images or information unless the victim pays money or provides more images. Under CMCA 2018 — up to 25 years imprisonment. Kutishia kushiriki picha au taarifa za karibu isipokuwa mwathirika alipe pesa au atoe picha zaidi. Chini ya CMCA 2018 — hadi miaka 25 gerezani. Kuthreat kushare intimate images au info unless victim alipe pesa au atoe more images. Chini ya CMCA 2018 — hadi miaka 25 gerezani.
🔑 Non-Consensual Image Sharing (Revenge Porn)🔑 Kushiriki Picha Bila Ridhaa (Filamu ya Kisasi)🔑 Non-Consensual Sharing (Revenge Porn) Sharing intimate images of a person without their consent. A criminal offence under CMCA 2018 regardless of whether the image was originally shared willingly. Kushiriki picha za karibu za mtu bila idhini yao. Ni kosa la jinai chini ya CMCA 2018 bila kujali kama picha iliwahi kushirikiwa kwa hiari. Kushare intimate images za mtu bila consent yao. Ni criminal offence chini ya CMCA 2018 regardless ya kama picha ilishirikiwa willingly awali.

🔐 Identity Theft & HackingWizi wa Utambulisho na UdukuziIdentity Theft & Hacking

Identity theft and hacking are increasingly common in Kenya — especially as more young people use mobile banking and social media. Wizi wa utambulisho na udukuzi vinazidi kuwa vya kawaida Kenya — hasa vijana wengi wanapotumia benki ya simu na mitandao ya kijamii. Identity theft na hacking zinazidi kuwa common Kenya — especially vijana wengi wanatumia mobile banking na social media.

🔑 Identity Theft🔑 Wizi wa Utambulisho🔑 Identity Theft Using someone else's personal information — ID number, phone number, bank details — to commit fraud or impersonate them online. Kutumia taarifa za kibinafsi za mtu mwingine — nambari ya kitambulisho, nambari ya simu, maelezo ya benki — kufanya udanganyifu au kujifanya wao mtandaoni. Kutumia personal info ya mtu mwingine — ID number, phone number, bank details — kufanya fraud au kujifanya wao online.
🔑 Account Hacking🔑 Udukuzi wa Akaunti🔑 Account Hacking Accessing someone's email, social media, or banking account without their permission. Criminals use stolen passwords, phishing links, or weak security settings. Kufikia barua pepe, mitandao ya kijamii, au akaunti ya benki ya mtu bila ruhusa yake. Wahalifu hutumia manenosiri yaliyoibiwa, viungo vya phishing, au mipangilio dhaifu ya usalama. Kuaccess email, social media, au banking account ya mtu bila permission yao. Criminals hutumia stolen passwords, phishing links, au weak security settings.

How it happens: weak passwords, phishing links, use of public WiFi, sharing login details with friends.Jinsi inavyotokea: manenosiri dhaifu, viungo vya phishing, kutumia WiFi ya umma, kushiriki maelezo ya kuingia na marafiki.Jinsi inavyohappen: weak passwords, phishing links, public WiFi, kushare login details na marafiki.

💡 How to Protect Your Accounts💡 Jinsi ya Kulinda Akaunti Zako💡 Jinsi ya Protect Accounts Zako Use strong, unique passwords (mix of letters, numbers, symbols — at least 8 characters). Enable 2-Factor Authentication (2FA). Never click suspicious links. Never use public WiFi for banking. Tumia manenosiri imara na ya kipekee (mchanganyiko wa herufi, nambari, ishara — angalau herufi 8). Wezesha Uthibitisho wa Hatua 2 (2FA). Usibonyeze viungo vinavyotia shaka. Usitumie WiFi ya umma kwa benki. Tumia strong, unique passwords (mix ya letters, numbers, symbols — angalau characters 8). Enable 2-Factor Authentication (2FA). Never click suspicious links. Never tumia public WiFi kwa banking.
⚠️ If Your Account Is Hacked⚠️ Akaunti Yako Ikidukuiwa⚠️ Account Yako Ikidukuliwa Change your password immediately. Enable 2FA. Report to the platform. Report to the DCI Cybercrime Unit. Badilisha nenosiri lako mara moja. Wezesha 2FA. Ripoti kwa jukwaa. Ripoti kwa Kitengo cha Uhalifu wa Mtandao cha DCI. Badilisha password yako mara moja. Enable 2FA. Report kwa platform.
