🚿 Hygiene & Self-CareUsafi na KujitunzaHygiene & Self-Care

Good hygiene during puberty protects your health and your confidence.Usafi mzuri wakati wa balehe unalinda afya yako na ujasiri wako.Good hygiene wakati wa puberty inalinda health yako na confidence yako.

  • Bathe or wash daily — sweat glands become more active during pubertyOga au nawa kila siku — tezi za jasho zinakuwa zikitengeneza zaidi wakati wa baleheOga au wash kila siku — sweat glands zinakuwa more active wakati wa puberty
  • Use clean underwear daily — change every dayVaa chupi safi kila siku — badilisha kila sikuVaa clean underwear kila siku — badilisha kila siku
  • For girls: use clean pads or sanitary cloths during menstruationKwa wasichana: tumia pedi safi au nguo za usafi wakati wa hedhiKwa wasichana: tumia clean pads au sanitary cloths wakati wa hedhi
  • For pimples: wash face gently twice a day — don't squeezeKwa chunusi: nawa uso kwa upole mara mbili kwa siku — usikandeKwa pimples: wash uso kwa upole mara mbili kwa siku — usikande
  • Dental hygiene: brush twice dailyUsafi wa meno: piga mswaki mara mbili kwa sikuDental hygiene: piga mswaki mara mbili kwa siku
